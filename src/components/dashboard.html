Our point system is set up to motivate and appreciate recycling work. Each type of recyclable material has a specific point value. For instance, recycling paper and plastic items will get you 2 points each. Glass and metal items earn 5 points each, while chemical items can earn you 10 points each. The more you recycle, the more points you accumulate. These points can be monitored in your personal dashboard, giving you a concrete way to see your environmental contribution. Keep up the good work and happy recycling!
