App Creation
How did we come up with the name?

Coming up with the name "Smart Recycling" involved careful consideration of the main features and advantages that our app offers.  We went through the following steps when choosing the name:

  • -Brainstorming: We took notes of concepts related to recycling, and waste management
  • -Focus on Core Functionality: Since our app revolves around image recognition, we wanted a name that emphasized this smart, tech-driven aspect.
    The word "smart" suggests intelligence, reflecting the AI component of our application.
    The word "recycling" suggests sustainability and environmental consciousness.
  • -Clarity And Simplicity: The name "Smart Recycling" is clear and simple, yet conveys exactly the app's idea.
  • -Market Research: We made sure the name wasn't in use, but resonates with our target audience.

Explanation of the logo

Our logo is the Recycling Triangle Symbol, but instead of arrows we have leaves, to emphasize on the eco-friendly aspect that our app is focusing on.

Colors and Typography

Starting off with the choice of color, we went for something that is associated to nature and growth. The dark grey helps in text readability, and serves as a neutral background when in dark mode.
Its combination with light green adds a balance, and a vibrant touch to the app.
The beautiful dark green gives depth and contrast when in light mode.
The use of green tones is very important to the user experience, as it creates a sense of calmness and harmony, and connection to nature.

We opted for a clean and modern aesthetic. Helvetica Neue is sophisticated, sans-serif, and easy to read without causing visual strain
