How did we come up with the name?

Coming up with the name "Smart Recycling" involved careful consideration of the main features and advantages that our app offers.  We went through the following steps when choosing the name:

  • -Brainstorming: We took notes of concepts related to recycling, and waste management
  • -Focus on Core Functionality: Since our app revolves around image recognition, we wanted a name that emphasized this smart, tech-driven aspect.

Environmental Pollution

Environmental contamination results from things that could be recycled, but ending up in landiflls or incinerators. Methane for example, a powerful greenhouse gas, is released during the decomposition of organic waste, and soil and groundwater are contaminated by the leaching of hazardous chemicals from electronics and other non-recycled materials. This pollution puts wildlife in danger, damages ecosystems, and endangers people's health.
